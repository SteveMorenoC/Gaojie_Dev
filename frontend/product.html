Product Description

Our gentle amino acid cleanser is formulated with a unique blend of natural amino acids that effectively remove impurities while maintaining your skin's natural moisture barrier. Perfect for daily use on all skin types, including sensitive skin.

What makes it special:

  • Amino Acid Technology: Gentle cleansing that won't strip your skin
  • pH Balanced: Maintains your skin's natural pH level
  • Sulfate-Free: No harsh sulfates that can cause irritation
  • Dermatologically Tested: Safe for sensitive skin

How to Use

  1. Wet your face with lukewarm water
  2. Apply a small amount to your palms and create a gentle lather
  3. Massage onto face in circular motions for 30 seconds
  4. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water
  5. Pat dry with a clean towel

Best used: Morning and evening as part of your skincare routine.
