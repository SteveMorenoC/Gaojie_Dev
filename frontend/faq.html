Skincare Advice

Start with the basics: gentle cleanser, moisturizer, and SPF during the day. Our Amino-Acid Cleanser and Hydrating Moisturiser are perfect starter products. Introduce new treatments gradually, one at a time.

Prevention is key! Start with antioxidants like Vitamin C in your 20s, and consider gentle retinol products in your late 20s or early 30s. Our Vitamin C Brightening Serum is an excellent first anti-aging product.

Most customers see improved skin texture within 7-14 days. Significant changes in fine lines, hyperpigmentation, or acne typically take 6-12 weeks of consistent use. Patience and consistency are key to beautiful skin!
